Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Urmila HRMS Official ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects user information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Location Information

We collect precise device location (GPS-based) only when a user performs Punch In or Punch Out actions.

  • Used strictly to verify employee presence at the assigned office location
  • Collected only after explicit user consent
  • Location is accessed only during punch actions
  • No background, continuous, or passive location tracking

b) Camera / Image Data

The device camera is used to capture images during Punch In / Punch Out for attendance verification purposes.

  • Images are securely uploaded to organization servers
  • Accessible only to authorized organization administrators
  • Not shared with third parties for marketing or advertising

c) Internet Usage

Internet permission is required to sync attendance data, upload images, and fetch HR-related information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Attendance and workforce verification
  • HR record management
  • Compliance with organizational policies
  • Security, audit, and fraud prevention

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Data may be shared only with:

  • The user’s employer or organization
  • Authorized administrators within the organization
  • Legal authorities if required by applicable law

We do not share user data with third-party advertisers or use data for targeted advertising.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. User Consent & Control

Users may grant or revoke permissions (such as location or camera) at any time through device settings. Certain features may not function correctly if required permissions are denied.

6. Data Retention

Attendance, location, and image data are retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill organizational HR requirements and legal obligations.

7. Children’s Privacy

Urmila HRMS Official is intended for use by employees and is not designed for children under the age of 18.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
