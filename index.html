Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Urmila HRMS Official ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects user data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

The app displays limited personal information provided by the organization, such as name, email address, and employee ID, for account identification and HR management purposes.

b) Location Information

We collect precise device location (GPS-based) only when a user performs Punch In or Punch Out.

  • Used solely to verify employee presence at the assigned workplace
  • Collected only after user consent
  • No background, continuous, or passive location tracking

c) Camera / Image Data

The app uses the device camera to capture photos during Punch In and Punch Out for attendance verification.

  • Images are securely uploaded to organization servers
  • The app does not browse the user’s photo gallery
  • Images are not used for advertising, marketing, or analytics

d) Documents for Leave Applications

Users may upload documents (such as medical certificates or supporting files) when applying for leave through the app.

  • Documents are uploaded securely to organization servers
  • Used only for leave approval and HR record purposes
  • Accessible only to authorized organizational administrators

e) Internet Usage

Internet access is required to authenticate users, sync attendance data, upload images and documents, and retrieve HR-related information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Attendance verification and workforce management
  • Leave processing and HR record maintenance
  • Account management and authentication
  • Security, audit, and fraud prevention

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Data may be shared only with:

  • The user’s employer or organization
  • Authorized HR administrators
  • Legal authorities if required by law

We do not share user data with third-party advertisers or use data for targeted advertising.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Data transmission is secured using industry-standard encryption.

5. User Consent and Control

The app requests permissions (such as location and camera) only when required for specific features. Users may manage or revoke permissions through their device settings. Some features may not function properly if required permissions are denied.

6. Data Retention

Personal information, attendance records, images, and documents are retained only as long as necessary to fulfill organizational HR requirements and applicable legal obligations.

7. Children’s Privacy

Urmila HRMS Official is intended for organizational employees and is not designed for children under the age of 18.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
