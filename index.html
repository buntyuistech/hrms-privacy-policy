Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Urmila HRMS Official ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects user data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

The app displays limited personal information provided by the organization, such as name, email address, and employee ID, solely for account identification and HR management purposes.

b) Location Information

We collect precise device location (GPS-based) only when a user performs Punch In or Punch Out.

  • Used solely to verify employee presence at the assigned workplace
  • Collected only after explicit user consent
  • No background, continuous, or passive location tracking

c) Camera / Attendance Photo

The app uses the device camera to capture an attendance photo during Punch In and Punch Out for attendance verification.

  • Photos are captured only when the user initiates Punch In/Punch Out
  • Photos are securely uploaded to organization servers
  • The app does not browse the user’s photo gallery
  • The app does not request broad access to the device photo/video library
  • Photos are not used for advertising, marketing, or analytics

d) Documents for Leave Applications

Users may upload supporting documents (such as medical certificates or other files) when applying for leave through the app. Documents are selected using a system file picker (for example, Android file picker).

  • The app accesses only the file(s) explicitly selected by the user
  • Documents are uploaded securely to organization servers
  • Documents are used only for HR record purposes and leave approval
  • Accessible only to authorized organizational administrators

e) Internet Usage

Internet access is required to authenticate users, sync attendance data, upload photos/documents, and retrieve HR-related information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Attendance verification and workforce management
  • Leave processing and HR record maintenance
  • Account authentication and access control
  • Security, audit, and fraud prevention

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Data may be shared only with:

  • The user’s employer or organization
  • Authorized HR administrators
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law

We do not share user data with third-party advertisers or use data for targeted advertising.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Data transmission is protected using industry-standard encryption.

5. Permissions and User Control

The app requests permissions only when required for specific features:

  • Location: Only during Punch In / Punch Out
  • Camera: Only for capturing attendance photos
  • Internet: For syncing HR and attendance data

The app does not request broad photo/video library access permissions. Users may manage or revoke permissions at any time through device settings.

6. Data Retention

Personal information, attendance records, photos, and documents are retained only for as long as necessary to meet organizational HR requirements and applicable legal obligations.

7. Children’s Privacy

Urmila HRMS Official is intended exclusively for organizational employees and is not designed for children under the age of 18.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
